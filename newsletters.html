Message from Ethan

My art is a reflection of my expressions and passion. I have passion when I am drawing, painting, or creating. Art is a place in my heart and each piece of art has a meaning to it.

With Thanksgiving coming up, I think about the importance of being with family. Therefore, I selected this picture of me and my future wife, kids, dogs and house. Family means the world to me. I look forward to one day starting a family and raisingchildren. We are heading off to Nicaragua for Thanksgiving where I will teach art to kids at a local school and we will donate art supplies to the school. Our Thanksgiving will be very special and I hope yours will be also! Happy Thanksgiving! - Ethan
